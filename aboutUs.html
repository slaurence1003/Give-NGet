About Us


Learn About Our Mission

Welcome to Give 'N Get', a passionate project initiated by six determined women on a mission to make a difference. As computer science students, we harnessed our technical skills and shared commitment to create a platform that connects those in need with vital resources. Our website is designed to empower and uplift women facing various challenges. We believe in the power of community and support, and that's why we've built a space where women can access essential resources and find the help they deserve. It also provides opportunity to give back to our communities. Together, we're creating a network of solidarity and kindness, striving to improve the lives of women in need. We hope you join us on this journey!
